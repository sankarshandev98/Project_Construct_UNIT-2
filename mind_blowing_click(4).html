What Kind of Sound Moves You?

Why do we ask?

Some headphones are tuned to appeal to specific sound signatures based on how they're made.

BALANCED

A neutral sound that doesn't emphasize on any one frequency. Genres: Everything, if you are a purist

V-SHAPED

An equal amount of bass & treble that adds excitement & is enjoyable. Genres: Rock, Pop, EDM, Bollywood

EXTRA BASS

Lots of low ends but tends to overpower mids & highs. Genres: EDM, Hip-Hop

FLAT

Designed for studio professionals for critical reference monitoring. Genres: All

WARM & SMOOTH

A little warmth in bass with natural vocals & treble
